  \textbf{Random Competitive Replication}

  
   
    $$  \frac{n_k}{n_{k-1}}
    =
    \frac{(k-1)(1-\simonalpha)}
    {1+(1-\simonalpha)k}
    $$ 
   
    Rearrange:
    $$
    = \frac{k(1-\simonalpha) - (1-\simonalpha)}
    {k(1-\simonalpha) + 1}
    $$
   
    Divide through by $k(1-\simonalpha)$:
    $$
    {\frac{1 - \frac{1}{k}}
    {{ 1 + \frac{1}{k(1-\simonalpha)}}}
    }
    $$
